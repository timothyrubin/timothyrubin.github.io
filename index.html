My main focus these days is working as the founder of Wellness Technology Inc., where I created Wellness AI, a mobile app that provides personalized guided meditations and AI therapy. Wellness AI is reimagining mental health support by combining two powerful approaches: adaptive AI-powered therapy sessions and an innovative system for generating personalized guided meditations. As the first app to create truly personal meditation experiences, Wellness AI learns about each user over time to provide support that's available whenever needed.

Previously, I was the VP of Data Science at Doma, a data science team lead at Change Healthcare, a senior data scientist at Surveymonkey, and a research scientist at Indiana University.
